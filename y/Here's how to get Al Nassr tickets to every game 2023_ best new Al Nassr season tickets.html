Your guide to getting Al Nassr season tickets

Here’s how to get tickets to every Al Nassr game at Alawwal Park

by Manal Al MotairiOctober 16, 2023October 18, 2023

Footie fans, buying Al Nassr tickets to every match just got easier. Al Nassr season tickets for the 2023-2024 season are here.

Al Nassr season tickets will get you access to all 17 games at Alawwal Park in the Saudi Pro League, aka the Roshn Saudi League.

The famous Saudi team has a full schedule of matches lasting starting in August so if you’re a supporter of the Cristiano Ronaldo-led team, listen up.

The popularity of football in the city has always been big, but with the GOAT joining the team last season, it has gotten a whole lot bigger.

How to get Al Nassr tickets:

Ready to cheer at the next Al Nassr game with your number 7 jersey at other games? Al Nassr season tickets start at SAR685 for Category Five tickets, and they go up to SAR131,175 for The Terrace tickets.

Al Nassr season ticket categories also include:

  • Category One (centre) from SAR2,315
  • Category Two (centre edges) for SAR1,435
  • Category Three (corners) for SAR1,015
  • Category Four (behind the goal) from SAR795
  • Category Five (behind goal fee) for SAR685
  • Sports Lounge for SAR8,545
  • Gallery Lounge for SAR10,525
  • Gallery Lounge Plus for SAR12,640
  • Platinum Lounge for SAR16,015
  • Alawwal Prime for SAR19,065

You can also pick up Al Nassr season tickets via tickets.victoryarena.com. You will need to sign in using your existing Victory Arena account or register for a new account through 

Alawwal Park is the official stadium of the Al Nassr team located at King Saud University (it was previously known as Mrsool Park and Victory Arena). The stadium is also where Cristiano Ronaldo’s unveiling took place, positioning him as the new captain of the yellow and blue team.

About Al Nassr FC

As mentioned, Al Nassr is a Saudi football club with an estimated 28 championships. Locally, it has won nine Premier League titles and two Saudi Super Cups, and counting. The club took to international heights with the official signing of Portuguese player Cristiano Ronaldo into the club for a-two year contract last season.

The colours of the team are yellow and blue and its home stadium is Alawwal Park based in Riyadh.

From SAR685. From Aug 18. Alawaal Park, King Saudi University, tickets.victoryarena.com.
